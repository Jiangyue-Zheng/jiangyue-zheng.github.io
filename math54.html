  • Some mistakes that appeared in your quiz, all of which are very important issues:
  • 1. The projection formula works on orthogonal basis only. If you apply the formula on a random basis, the result will be wrong! The bases we give in WS#21 are all orthogonal bases, so you don’t need to do anything. But if in a question(like the quiz#9 question 2(b) for section 117), the basis is not otrhogonal, you have to change the basis to orthogonal basis by GPS first. (Page 6&7 in October 28th slides explain this problem).
  • 2. To find the image of a matrix A: first find the pivot columns of EF(A), then take the correlated original cols of A to form Im(A). Do not take the cols in EF(A) to form Im(A), they are different! (Page 3 in October 28th slides explain this problem).
